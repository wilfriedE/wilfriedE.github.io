Some active Projects

FIRSTMastery

FIRSTMastery is a crowdsourced learning platform currently under development. The goal is to have an easily accessible platform where all can contribute. With Lesson versioning, people can consume the latest best materials. With the flexibility of the content types, any kind of material can be used to teach such as PDFs, Videos, Powerpoints, Games etc..

Check it out

(*rails-implementation* is the current active branch )

LearnIt

LearnIt is the main implementation of the learning platform that FIRSTMastery will diverge from. LearnIt will become an upstream of FIRSTMastery and anyone who wants to run a similar platform can just setup LearnIt and reconfigure it to their own settings.
